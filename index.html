M — Mechanism
The ONE biological reason Keaps works. Pick one (never stack two).
M1
DHA delivery gap (BBB)Brain can't make DHA; most omega-3 forms can't cross the blood-brain barrier in usable amounts. Keaps delivers the form that does.
M2
Resolvins / protectinsInflammation must be actively resolved, not just suppressed. DHA fuels the resolution pathway.
M3
Cortisol → hippocampal atrophyChronic stress shrinks the hippocampus. Lower cortisol response = neurogenesis can restart.
M4
Mitochondrial neuron energyBrain cells run on ATP; aging mitochondria make less. The fog is an energy problem.
M5
Synaptic plasticity / BDNFDecline = losing the ability to form new connections. BDNF is the signal; DHA is the lumber.
M6
Cerebral blood flowTiny brain vessels stiffen; red cells need DHA in their membrane to fold and squeeze through to the deep brain.
M7
Sebum-rusting / nonenal (age-odor)Different product angle: skin oils oxidize into "old-person smell"; omega-3 supports the oxidative balance.
L — Why it's different from normal DHA
The product-bridge lever. Pick ONE and rotate it across the campaign (don't stack).
L1
Source (Mother Source)Fish only have DHA because they eat algae. Skip the middleman, go to the source.
L2
Oxidation / freshnessMost fish oil is half-rancid before you swallow it. The burp is your body rejecting it. Algae is fresh.
L3
Form / shapeCheap fish oil is the wrong molecular form; the brain barely recognizes it. The form matters more than the dose.
L4
Absorption (EQUAL)Algal DHA is proven non-inferior to fish oil. The win is the clean source — NOT a multiplier. Never say "8x".
L5
DoseMost omega-3 is underdosed vs the amount used in studies. A dose too small does nothing.
L6
Purity / toxinsFish carry mercury, PCBs, microplastics. Algae is grown clean on land — none of that.
L7
DHA-vs-EPAMost "omega-3" is EPA-heavy (heart). The brain is built from DHA. You fed your heart and starved your head.
L8
Synthetic / fillerMuch of the category is synthetic or fairy-dusted. Keaps states the dose plainly, no proprietary blend.
